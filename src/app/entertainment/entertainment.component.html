Entertainment

Arcade
We are located at 12345 Meriton Landing and are open from 9am to 9pm. There a many games and activities to pick from, for all ages including adults! Come and enjoy a great time at our local arcade, where kids can have a world of fun.
Helicopter Rides
We are located at 55555 Merriton Landing and are open from the hours of 7am to 9pm. View the Island from above in our helicopter rides. Rides are $50 per person and $25 per child under the age of 12.
Bowling
We are located at 122222 Baker St and are open from the hours of 12pm to 8pm. Enjoy a night of bowling. Prices are $20 per adult, and $12 per child under the age of 16.
Tropical Rain Dance Club
We are located at 124443 Baker St and are open from the hours of 12pm to 12am. Enjoy a night of dancing with your friends. family, and maybe someone new. Prices are $20 per adult, and you must be 18 years of age or older to attend.
Microbrewery
We are located at 444555 Wall St and are open from the hours of 9am to 6pm. Make new cocktails, try our fan favorites, and more at our local microbrewery. Please call ahead to make a reservation at (719) 597-5678. Pricing varies by party size.
Allen's Pub
We are located at 14599 Winery Circle and are open from the hours of 12pm to 1am. Make new cocktails, try our fan favorites, and more at our local pubs. Allen's was established in 1958 and is the oldest and most influential pub in Taniti. Please call ahead to make a reservation at (719) 597-5678. Pricing varies by party size.
