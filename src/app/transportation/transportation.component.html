Transportation

Air
Our local airport can support small jets and propeller planes, as well as commercial flights that are scheduled.
Buses
Public buses: Buses are running from 5am-11pm.
Private Buses: Are available for rent.
Boat
The main dock receives one ship per week to deliver travelers from the main land.
Rental Cars
Are for rent near the airport, rates vary depending on availability and car.

Bikes
Are available for rent, both adult and children sizes are available. Helmets are required by law.
