Sight Seeing

Taniti City
Our small and secluded city has lots of beautiful architecture and amazing views of the island. Taniti historically produced lots of fish and agricultural products.

Hiking and Adventure
Our more popular hikes are our guided tours into our tropical rain forest and our guided tours up to our active volcano. To schedule a tour please visit our adventure page. Explore our wonderful local rain forest, which includes many species of bird, frog and more. Explore our local volcano, which includes lots of volcanic rock, amazing sights, and more.
Yellow Leaf Bay
Would you like to relax on a white sandy beach? Well look no further, as our Yellow Leaf Bay has many beaches to choose from. For more information about our beaches visit our adventure page. Enjoy some family time while swimming in the water, or just relaxing in the sand.
Local History Museum
We are located at 2123 Merriton Landing, and are open from 9am to 7pm.

Our local history museum costs $15 per adult, and $5 per child. Tickets can be bought at the entrance with cash or card.
