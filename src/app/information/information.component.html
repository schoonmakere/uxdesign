Island Information

About Taniti

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000.

Emergency Preparedness

There is one hospital and serveral clinics in the area. The hospital has many multilingual employees. If there is an emergency call 111.

Violent Crime is very rare on Tanitit, but as tourism increases, the rate of pickpocketing and other petty crimes is increasing

Hurricans and Tidal Waves do happen on occasion, please view safety documents before visiting .

General Information

Power: Power outlets are 120 volts, sam eas in America.

Drinking Age: Drinking age is 18 yrs only, however it is not strictly enforced. Alcohol: Alcohol is not served between the hours of 12am and 9am.

Currency: Uses US dollar as its main currency, however many business will accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency change, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
